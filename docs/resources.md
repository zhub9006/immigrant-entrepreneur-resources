A curated list of organizations, programs, and tools specifically designed to support immigrant and refugee entrepreneurs.

## Government Programs

- **SBA Office of Entrepreneurial Development** — Free counseling and training for small business owners
- **SBA 8(a) Business Development Program** — Assistance for socially and economically disadvantaged businesses
- **State-level occupational licensing reform** — Several states (including Colorado) have removed barriers for undocumented entrepreneurs
- **Community Development Financial Institutions (CDFIs)** — Alternative lending for those without traditional credit history

## Key Statistics to Know

- Immigrants and their children make up **27%** of the U.S. population but have founded **46%** of Fortune 500 companies
- Those Fortune 500 companies generated **$8.6 trillion** in revenue in FY 2023
- **80%** of billion-dollar startups have a first- or second-generation immigrant founder or senior leader
- Immigrant-founded businesses **grow faster** and **last longer** than native-born-founded companies
- Nearly **1 in 5** small business owners across the U.S. and Europe are immigrant entrepreneurs
