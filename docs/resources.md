## 📊 Key Statistics

Understanding the economic impact of immigrant entrepreneurship helps you advocate for your business and your community:

- **46%** of Fortune 500 companies were founded by immigrants or their children
- **$8.6 trillion** in annual revenue from immigrant-founded Fortune 500 companies (FY 2023)
- **80%** of billion-dollar U.S. startups have immigrant founders or key leaders
- Immigrants start **20%** of all new U.S. businesses despite being only **14%** of the population
- Immigrant-founded businesses **grow faster** and **survive longer** than native-founded counterparts
- **823,000+** undocumented small business owners contribute to the U.S. economy
- Immigrant women are **starting businesses at 2x the rate** of native-born women
